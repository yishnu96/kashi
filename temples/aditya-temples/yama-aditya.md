# Yama Aditya

Yama Aditya in Kashi is the Surya form consecrated by Yamraj at Yam Teerth, where Kashi Khanda assures freedom from the torments of Yamaloka for those who bathe at Sankata Ghat and worship Yameshwar and Yama Aditya with faith. It is one of the Dwadasha Aditya shrines, set between Yameshwar and Atma Veereshwar, and is revered for Pitru-karya, happiness, and safe passage after death as described in Kashi Khandaâ€™s Aditya section.
