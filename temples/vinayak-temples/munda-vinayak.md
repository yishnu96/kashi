Munda Vinayak is a revered form of Shri Ganesh, associated with the Chandi Devi Temple, signifying the power of Ganesh to remove obstacles and grant strength. Devotees visit Munda Vinayak to seek blessings for overcoming challenges, particularly those that require a strong will and determination.
